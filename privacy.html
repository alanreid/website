3. Legal Bases

We process personal data on the following legal bases under the GDPR:

  • Pre-contractual measures (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR) — processing is necessary to take steps at your request prior to entering into a contract, such as responding to a contact form inquiry.
  • Legitimate interests (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR) — processing is necessary for our legitimate interests, such as providing and securing this website.

The legal basis for processing inquiries by email or phone is Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR (pre-contractual measures) or Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR (legitimate interest in responding to inquiries).

When you use the contact form, the following data is transmitted:

You may request the deletion of your contact data at any time by contacting us at the details above, subject to any statutory retention obligations.

Your contact data will be deleted once your inquiry has been fully handled, unless statutory retention obligations apply (e.g. 6 years under § 257 HGB, 10 years under § 147 AO for tax-relevant correspondence).
